Following the success of the previous conference series, the 15th International Conference on Atomically Controlled Surfaces, Interfaces and Nanostructures (ACSIN-15) will be held in SUZHOU, China.

This conference is a series of international conferences held every two years. It was initiated and held firstly in 1991 in Tokyo, supported by the Thin Film and Surface Physics Division in The Japan Society of Applied Physics. In the past 32 years, ACSIN has been successfully hosted in Japan, Sweden, Spain, Italy and France. In 2023, Suzhou, Jiangsu Province, was finally selected as the host city for the 15th ACSIN. More than 1,000 researchers from around the world will disseminate the latest advances in science and technology.

Beihang University will be the host of this conference, together with co-organiser universities including: University of Shanghai for Science and Technology.

This multidisciplinary conference will cover all the topics related to nanoscience and nanotechnology, surfaces and interface science, and physical chemistry. The topics include advances in surface characterization, surface nanotechnology and devices, energy, environment, catalysis, and bio-medical, batteries and energy storage, low-dimensional materials and quantum matters, and modeling and simulation.

The conference will bring together scientists in broad fields, such as condensed matter physics, surface science, molecular physics, catalytic science, inorganic and organic chemistry, biology, chemical and environmental engineering. Young scientists and students are strongly encouraged to present their research work, build networks and facilitate their career development in ACSIN. The 15th ACSIN is one of the most important forums for the dissemination and discussion of latest scientific advances in the broad field of science, engineering and applications. The conference, its participants and topics covered, will certainly provide new insights and directions taken by world leading scientists to address emerging problems in the fields of functional materials, surface physics and chemistry, renewable energy, environment protection, catalysis, and bio-medical applications. On behalf of the organizing committee, we would like to invite you to join us for the 15th International Conference on Atomically Controlled Surfaces, Interfaces and Nanostructures (ACSIN-15).

    Requirements for Call for Papers:

  • Papers that have been published in journals or presented at national conferences are not eligible.
  • All papers should be edited in Word, and the files should be saved in DOC format, and the format is referred to the paper template.
  • Submit papers (abstracts) on the official website of the conference - My Participation - Report Manuscript Each participant can submit one abstract. The submission period is from January 22, 2024 to April 20, 2024.
  • When submitting papers, please indicate the sub-item of the content of the paper to which the paper belongs, and paper manuscripts will not be accepted.
